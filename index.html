FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Here are the top questions our clients ask before getting started.

What is FarmGen and who is it for?

FarmGen is a next-generation agricultural technology platform designed for farmers, agri-input companies, and rural innovators. It offers AI-powered farming assistants, autonomous drones and robots, and a smart data platform to improve decision-making, productivity, and connectivity across the entire agri-ecosystem.

How does the FarmGen Agricultural Assistant work?

FarmGen’s assistant is an AI-powered virtual guide that provides personalized crop advice, pest alerts, market price updates, weather warnings, and more. It supports voice and text in regional Indian languages and can even work offline, ensuring every farmer has access to expert guidance — anytime, anywhere.

Do I need technical knowledge to use FarmGen drones or robots?

Not at all. Our drones and robots are designed to be farmer-friendly. We provide full setup, training, and ongoing support. Most tasks are automated and can be operated through a simple mobile interface. We also offer leasing options and community sharing models for cost-effective access.

Is FarmGen available in all regions of India?

Yes! FarmGen is expanding rapidly across India. Our assistant supports multiple Indian languages, and our drone/robot services are available in most states. If your region isn't covered yet, you can still register your interest and we’ll prioritize deployment based on demand.
